About Verma Medical Store

Established in 2002, Verma Medical Store has been serving the community with genuine medicines and healthcare products. Our mission is to provide accessible healthcare solutions with compassion and professionalism.

We stock a comprehensive range of prescription drugs, over-the-counter medications, surgical supplies, and wellness products from reputable pharmaceutical companies.

Our team of qualified pharmacists and staff are committed to ensuring you receive the right medications and proper guidance for your health needs.
